# Contributing Guidelines

We welcome contributions to the Club Entrepreneur Library Manager from all Pôle Léonard de Vinci students!

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the repository** on GitHub.
2. **Clone your fork** locally.
3. **Create a branch** for your feature or bugfix (`git checkout -b feat/my-new-feature`).
4. **Make your changes**, ensuring you follow the architectural separation (UI in `pages/`, queries in `database.py`).
5. **Commit your changes** using Conventional Commits (`feat: added X`, `fix: resolved Y`).
6. **Push to your fork** and open a Pull Request against the `main` branch.

Please keep Pull Requests small and focused on a single issue.
